What Are Slime Molds?

Slime molds (Physarum polycephalum) are fascinating, single-celled organisms that exhibit intelligent behavior despite lacking a brain. They move, solve mazes, and form intricate networks to find food. This visualizer replicates their movements using a custom algorithm. You can customize the color, background, and size of the slime molds to create mesmerizing and unique patterns.
